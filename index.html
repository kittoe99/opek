Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from Denver homeowners.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. Many clients provide entry instructions so we can clean while they’re away. We always secure your home before we leave.

What should I do before my appointment?

Ensure pets are safe and remove personal items from surfaces you’d like cleaned. We’ll take care of the rest.

How do I book recurring cleanings?

When you reach out, let us know your ideal cadence—weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly—and we’ll reserve your preferred time slot.
